HLIFE

There is no exclusive platform where people can store all of their medical records or directly share/accept medical records from their doctors. hLife is a simple, easy to use iOS mobile app, where people can store their medical records.

THE CHALLENGE

Lab reports, medical prescriptions and history are stored at different doctor’s portal, hard copies or buried in our email somewhere. We have to call our previous doctor to get copies of our medical reports emailed to us. This makes it really hard especially when we change our doctors or move to a new location.

THE SOLUTION

hLife. A cloud storage platform exclusively to store medical records and track history.

hLife makes it really easy for you to store medical records and accept records from your doctor.

USER SURVERY

TESTING HLIFE

I conducted a user survey to gather insights on what problems users are facing in current cloud storage platforms. I also wanted to understand what users think about using a medical cloud storage platform to store their medical records and also how they currently stored/shared their medical records with their doctors. I sent the survey out to discovery forums and friends.

MARKET CONFIRMATION

From the survey results we were able to confirm that people use antiquated methods to store their medical records such as emails and hard copies. People are looking for a secure platform to store their medical records.

HARD COPIES & EMAILS

80% of the respondents "store their medical records in emails or as hard copies"

STORE RECORDS

65% of the respondents "feel it is important for them to store their medical records"

ONE PLATFORM

80% of the respondents "feel it is important to store their records in one platform"

MULTIPLE ACCOUNTS

47% of the respondents "have multiple accounts in different doctors platform to maintain their records"

SHARE RECORDS

77% of the respondents "want a platform that enables them to be able to share their medical records"

PERSONA

WHO WILL USE HLIFE?

Keeping in mind the results from our user survey, I created fictional characters to represent what kind of users would use our product. I wanted to remind myself of the needs, behaviours and user goals and these personas help me put myself in the user's shoes and I can make sure the user’s perspectives are taken into consideration.

SEAN PLAICE

“I’m looking for a one stop platform where I can store, view & share my and my family’s medical history”

  • Age: 38
  • Work: Software Engineer
  • Family: Married, 2 Kids
  • Location: Seattle, WA
  • Outdoor Games, Sports is Life
  • Stay Positive, Fitness & Health
  • Techie & Nature Lover

Goals

  • Maintain his family’s medical record history
  • Find a one-stop location to store and view medical records history
  • Be able to share medical reports with doctors & family

Frustrations

  • Every doctor has a separate portal
  • Hardcopies are hard to maintain and keep track of
  • Can't remember the medical history of his kids
  • Visiting multiple doctors making it hard to share medical reports

LAURA KOTLIKOFF

“I want to maintain a seperate heath record file for each family member and want to digitalize everything”

  • Age: 46
  • Work: Lawyer
  • Family: Married, 3 Kids
  • Location: Seattle, WA
  • Cooking and Baking
  • Taking care of her kids
  • Traveling around

Goals

  • Wants to maintain a separate health record file for each of her family member
  • Wants to be able to easily segregate health records into files/folders
  • Maintain copies of health records digitally

Frustrations

  • Hard copies are hard to maintain and time-consuming
  • All her families medical records are mixed together
  • Can't remember the heath histroy for each family member
